About Us

Hello and welcome to 263 hit Radio Radio (263 hit Radio) the radio Station of Choice where people with like minded ideas meet and mingle where passion meets purpose we bring you the best in music, entertainment and education.

Founded in November 2024, 263 hit Radio Radio was created to bring together joy, love and happiness to its fans. Whether you're tuning in for the music, the stories, or the vibes, you're part of a community that celebrates and connects everyone. Our live broadcasting starts from 6pm GMT daily.

Give us a try and let us know what you think. 263 hit Radio Radio - a Station of Pure love and Entertainment.
